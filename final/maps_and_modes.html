Payload

One of the most popular gamemodes. BLU players have to push a cart through the map, while RED players have to stop them.

Competitive versions often utilize Stopwatch Mode - where one team simply has to capture more points faster than the other.

    Some popular maps are...

  • Upward: BLU team fights up a mountain culimating in an indoor battle at RED's base.
  • Badwater: BLU and RED engage over a wide area with a large variety of terrain for both teams to utilize.

KOTH

King of the Hill, a well known gamemode where both teams fight to control a central control point for as long as possible.

Generally, maps are symmetric on both sides.

    Some popular maps are...

  • Viaduct: Teams fight to control the point at a central hill, with both low and high ground at each side to be used strategically.
  • Lakeside: This map is well known for its central pillars and unique design and layout. It is often used with the Highlander ruleset.

Control Points

One of the most played gamemodes within the competitive community and often shortened to 5CP, this mode has both teams fight to capture all of the points on the map.

Similar to KOTH, the maps are generally symmetric as well.

    Some popular maps are...

  • Badlands: With a wide variety of high and low ground, as well as external and internal areas, Badlands is one of the more well known 5CP maps.
  • Process: Probably the most well known due to its popular rollouts, Process has lot of options for movement and combat. It one of the most popular competitive maps.

Attack/Defend

Similar to both Control Points and Payload, the BLU team attempts to capture all of the points while the RED team has to stop them.

There are a wide variety of layouts, with the most variety out of any other gamemode.

    Some popular maps are...

  • Mountain Lab: Taking place in an area of the same name, this map takes the BLU team from a grassy hill to the heart of RED's base.
  • DeGroot Keep: Taking place within a medieval castle, this map uses the Medieval Mode ruleset, which forces everyone to use (mostly) only melee weapons.

CTF

CTF plays the same as the classic game entails: one team has to steal a briefcase from the enemy base and bring it back to their own.

It is probably the most well known gamemode due to the infamous 2fort.

    Some popular maps are...

  • 2fort: Perhaps the perfect embodiement of Team Fortress 2, each team has a fort and has to assault the enemy fort while defending their own from invaders.
  • Turbine: Another well known CTF map, Turbine takes place entirely indoors and is full of tight, claustrophobic spaces perfect for close quarters combat.
