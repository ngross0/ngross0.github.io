Offense Classes || Defense Classes || Support Classes || Maps and Modes

The Offense Class catagory includes the Scout, Soldier, and Pyro.

They posses good health, mobility, or both making them generally well suited for fighting at the front.

Flanking and ambushes are also effective strategies these classes excel at.

The Defense Class catagory includes the Demoman, Heavy, and Engineer.

They posses high health, and strong defensive options, but relatively low mobility.

In addition to being effective defenders, these classes can also be strong on the front line.

The Support Class catagory includes the Medic, Sniper, and Spy.

Instead of fighting at the front themselves, they are more suited to supporting from the back.

A single well timed action from these classes has the potential to shift the tides of battle instantly.

Team Fortress 2 has many gamemodes, from the well-loved Payload to Pass Time, and may more.

Some of the more popular ones are discussed within.
