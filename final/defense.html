Information about the Heavy class goes here.

    • With the highest health pool and one of the highest DPS weapons in the game, the Heavy makes for a great asset on any team.
    • His slow movement speed is his biggest setback, making it difficult for him to arrive at a battlefield at the same pace as his allies.
    • This also makes him a prime target for Snipers and Spies, both of which are able to take him out with a single blow.
    • Heavies paired up with Medics are a classic image, for good reason. Invulnerable Heavies are a nearly unstopable offensive or defensive force.
    • Additonally, the Heavy posses secondary weapons that can be used to heal his teammates, keeping Medics or other allies alive in a fight.

Information about the Engineer class goes here.

    • The Engineer can build a variety of buildings to support his team. He must manage his metal wisely to keep them operating properly.
    • Sentry Guns are good for defending key areas and can deal a lot of damage very quickly. They can even be used offensively which smart placement.
    • Dispensers are invauluable for keeping a team at full health and ammo, and should be positioned somewhere near the front where teammates can quickly use it.
    • Teleporters can quickly move allies to the front lines, useful for maintaining a well-fortified defensive line, or for keeping constant offensive pressure.
    • Stationary buildings are an easy target for enemies, so the Engineer is most effective when supported by his teammates.
